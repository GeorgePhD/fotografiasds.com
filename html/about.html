"Immense passion for nature and its landscapes"

I have traveled through different parts of Chile, the north desert, the boundless coast of the Pacific Ocean, the high peaks of the Andes Mountains, the dense forests of the central south and the endless Patagonia with its gigantic glaciers and ceaseless lakes.

I enjoy being able to capture moments and landscapes to create authentic images that manage to convey the excitement of nature, its flora and fauna. I am motivated to learn and improve myself to continue studying the path of photography together with my career that allows me to explore and enjoy what I love the most.

I always try to listen and learn from the experience of other people, enriching the practice and generating meaningful connections with whom I interact.

Welcome to the world of breathtaking landscapes and captivating moments captured through the lens of a passionate photographer!

My photographic work dates back to 2015 where ,for the first time, I stepped on the famous lands of Patagonia. A trip that I made autonomously, alone with my sports camera. It was at that moment where I decided to look for something that would help me to return, to work, to stay, to enjoy in this wonderful place, The Eighth Wonder Of The World. With a keen eye for detail and an innate ability to capture the essence of a scene.

My photographs not only depict breathtaking landscapes but also convey the emotions and stories hidden within nature's embrace.

In addition to my photographic endeavors, I am also a professional tourist guide. I possess extensive knowledge about the hidden treasures and secrets of the remarkable Torres del Paine National Park, ensuring an unforgettable experience for those who embark on guided tours with me. Whether you are a seasoned traveler or a first-time adventurer, I will take you on an immersive journey, unveiling the wonders of Torres del Paine in a unique and insightful way.

My expertise and passion for nature will undoubtedly enhance your exploration, allowing me to appreciate the park's awe-inspiring beauty and discover hidden gems that are often overlooked.

My portfolio showcases a diverse range of photographs, each one telling a story and evoking emotions within the viewer. From majestic mountain peaks to serene lakes, from vibrant flora to elusive wildlife, every image is a testament to my deep connection with the natural world.

Browse through my captivating collection, and allow yourself to be transported to the pristine landscapes of Chile. With every click, you will experience the raw beauty and untamed wilderness that captivated me.

Guided Tours: Experience the wonders of Torres del Paine National Park through the eyes of a professional tourist guide. Discover hidden trails, encounter elusive wildlife, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Fine Art Prints: Bring the beauty of nature into your own space with exquisite fine art prints. Each print is a testament to their artistic vision and meticulous attention to detail, allowing you to showcase the magnificence of Chilean landscapes in your home or office.
